## Background

Our client, a globally renowned Tier-1 automotive parts supplier, operates 12 production facilities across China, Southeast Asia, and Europe with annual exports exceeding  million. Facing increasingly stringent JIT delivery requirements from OEMs, their decentralized supply chain model struggled with slow inventory turnover, high transport costs, and volatile on-time delivery rates.

## Core Challenges

- **Multi-Plant Coordination**: Independent procurement and logistics management without unified scheduling
- **Limited Mode Mix**: Over 70% of international transport relied on ocean freight alone
- **Data Silos**: Non-interoperable ERP, WMS, and TMS systems
- **Port Clearance Bottlenecks**: Average 3-5 day clearance time at major export ports

## Solution

### Supply Chain Control Tower

Built a unified control tower platform integrating ERP systems across all production bases for real-time visibility into orders, inventory, and logistics. The platform includes anomaly detection that automatically triggers alerts and recommends corrective actions.

### Multimodal Transport Design

Differentiated transport strategies by product line and destination:

- **Europe JIT Production Parts**: Ocean + China Railway Express, 18-day delivery
- **Southeast Asia Finished Goods**: Truck + ocean combination, 7-day delivery
- **Emergency Replenishment**: Air freight, 48-hour delivery

### Port Clearance Acceleration

Deployed dedicated customs teams at major export ports, pre-completing HS code classification and documentation to compress clearance from 3-5 days to under 12 hours.

## Results

- 28% reduction in total logistics costs
- 55% improvement in inventory turnover
- On-time delivery rate from 82% to 97%
- 85% improvement in customs clearance efficiency
- ~ annual logistics cost savings
