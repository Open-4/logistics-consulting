## Background

An auto parts manufacturer with 60% of export revenue from North America faced slow supply chain response and inventory buildup as demand grew.

## Challenges

Order-to-delivery cycles of 45-60 days were causing lost orders. Slow-moving SKU inventory days exceeded 200 days. Demand forecast accuracy was below 60%.

## Solution

Redesigned the North American network with two distribution centers (LA and NJ) for zone-splitting. Implemented VMI with top three distributors. Deployed ML-based demand forecasting.

## Results

- 40% improvement in inventory turnover (4.5x to 6.3x)
- 60% reduction in stockout rate (8% to 3.2%)
- 35% shorter order-to-delivery cycle
- 25% warehousing cost reduction
