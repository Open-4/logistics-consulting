## Background
A consumer electronics company with + annual revenue faced slow inventory turnover and high operating costs across 7 APAC warehouses.
## Solution
- Identified 3 optimal regional hub locations through network modeling
- Redesigned inventory layout with centralized slow-movers and distributed fast-movers
- Implemented WMS for cross-warehouse inventory visibility
## Results
- 40% improvement in inventory turnover
- 25% reduction in annual logistics costs
- 30% shorter order fulfillment time
