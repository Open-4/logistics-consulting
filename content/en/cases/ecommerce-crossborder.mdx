## Background

Our client, a fast-growing DTC smart home brand with + GMV, faced significant fulfillment challenges as they expanded from US/Europe into Southeast Asia and the Middle East. Their single-carrier, domestic-direct-ship model struggled with high costs, slow delivery, and complex returns management.

## Core Challenges

- **High Fulfillment Costs**: Last-mile delivery costs exceeded 35% of total logistics spend
- **Slow Delivery**: 10-15 day average delivery to US/Europe vs. 3-5 days for Amazon FBA
- **Difficult Returns**: No local return addresses; return rates exceeding 20% with goods destroyed
- **Multi-Market Complexity**: Divergent tariff policies, restricted items, and consumer protection laws

## Solution

### Overseas Warehouse Network

Established 5 overseas warehouses across North America (LA, NJ), Europe (Frankfurt, Birmingham), and Southeast Asia (Singapore) with a tiered inventory strategy:

- **Fast-Movers**: Stocked in every regional warehouse for 3-day delivery
- **Slow-Movers**: Centralized in 1-2 regional warehouses
- **New Products**: Single-warehouse testing before regional rollout

### Smart Routing Engine

Developed an intelligent routing engine that considers inventory location, delivery address, time requirements, and cost to calculate optimal fulfillment paths in real-time. The system auto-compares carrier rates through API integration.

### Localized Returns Management

Established return processing centers at each warehouse for inspection, refurbishment, and resale. Non-resalable items are processed through local recycling channels.

## Results

- Average delivery time from 12 days to 4 days
- 40% reduction in last-mile delivery costs
- Overseas warehouse fulfillment from 0% to 65%
- 50% reduction in return processing costs
- Resale rate increased to 75%
