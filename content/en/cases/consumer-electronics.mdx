description: Established regional distribution network for a smart audio brand, reducing costs by 22% and improving delivery by 35%
---
## Background

A smart audio accessories brand with annual exports of ~CNY 200M was expanding into Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia and Malaysia in 2025. However, Southeast Asia''s fragmented logistics infrastructure and inconsistent customs processes became major obstacles.

## Challenges

The brand lacked logistics partner networks in Southeast Asia. Customs efficiency varied widely across countries. Indonesia''s inter-island logistics were complex and costly.

## Solution

Established a Regional Distribution Center (RDC) in Singapore as the hub for Southeast Asian distribution. Standardized customs documentation prepared in Singapore for each destination country. Selected local logistics partners in each country managed through unified KPIs.

## Results

- 22% logistics cost reduction through consolidated shipping
- 35% faster delivery from 7 days to 2-3 days
- Improved customer satisfaction scores from 4.2 to 4.5
- Inventory turnover improved from 3x to 5x annually
