## Background

A cross-border e-commerce seller with CNY 400M annual sales across Amazon, eBay, Wish and their own website faced chaotic logistics management.

## Challenges

Using 6 different carriers across platforms made management complex and costly. Logistics costs reached 28% of sales vs industry average of 15-20%. Staff spent 3 hours daily on logistics issues.

## Solution

Unified all carriers into a single management platform. Developed differentiated strategies per platform (FBA for Amazon, 3PL for eBay, hybrid for own site). Built data-driven replenishment planning.

## Results

- 18% logistics cost reduction
- 28% improvement in customer satisfaction
- 60% reduction in logistics processing time
- 90% reduction in shipping errors (3% to 0.3%)
