## Background
A leading automotive parts exporter with  annual exports faced an 8% customs inspection rate.
## Solution
- Conducted comprehensive HS code audit, correcting 72 misclassifications
- Built internal trade compliance management system
- Guided AEO Advanced Certification application
## Results
- Customs inspection rate dropped from 8% to 0.5%
- Clearance time reduced by 60%
- Approximately  annual duty savings
