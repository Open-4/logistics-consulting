## Background
A cross-border e-commerce company with + GMV serving the European market faced long transit times and peak-season capacity shortages.
## Solution
- Designed combined ocean + China Railway Express transport solution
- Established carrier tiering system for peak-season capacity assurance
- Developed smart route selection system
## Results
- Average transit time reduced from 35 to 18 days
- 22% reduction in per-container transport costs
- Peak-season capacity assurance rate increased to 95%
