## Market Overview
Container freight rates are expected to remain stable in Q3 2026, though geopolitical tensions continue to introduce uncertainty.
## Key Trends
- **Capacity**: New vessel deliveries increasing, but Red Sea diversions absorb some capacity
- **Demand**: US and Europe restocking cycles nearing completion
- **Rate Outlook**: Major route rates to fluctuate minimally; sharp increases unlikely
## Recommendations
We recommend medium-to-long term contracts with carriers to lock in rates, and diversified transport combinations to mitigate risk.
