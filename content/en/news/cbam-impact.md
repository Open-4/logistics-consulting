## CBAM Progress
The CBAM transition period is halfway through; carbon tariff implementation is approaching fast.
## Logistics Impact
- **Emissions Accounting**: Sea and air freight emissions included in product carbon footprint
- **Mode Selection**: Low-carbon transport gains cost advantage
- **Data Reporting**: Full-chain carbon data collection and reporting required
## Action Roadmap
1. Assess current product carbon footprint baseline
2. Optimize transport routes and modal mix
3. Build internal carbon management system
4. Explore green fuels and carbon offsets
