## Policy Highlights
New customs regulations for cross-border e-commerce retail exports raise the bar on declaration, tracking, and returns.
## Key Changes
- **Declaration**: More detailed product descriptions and HS codes required
- **Tracking**: End-to-end logistics traceability required
- **Returns**: Standardized return processing procedures
## Compliance Tips
Upgrade ERP systems to meet new customs requirements and build professional customs compliance teams.
