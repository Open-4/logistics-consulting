## Overview

In an increasingly complex global trade environment, customs compliance has become a core competitive advantage for cross-border businesses. We provide comprehensive customs compliance services to reduce risk, optimize duty costs, and improve clearance efficiency.

## Core Services

### HS Code Classification & Audit

HS code accuracy directly impacts duty rates and regulatory requirements. Our experts systematically review product catalogs to ensure correct and consistent classification, preventing penalties and delays from misclassification.

### AEO Certification Guidance

AEO certification is the key to customs facilitation. We provide end-to-end support from gap analysis to formal application:

- **Assessment**: Current-state gap analysis against AEO standards
- **System Building**: Establish internal trade compliance management systems
- **Documentation**: Prepare application materials and supporting documents
- **Mock Audit**: Conduct internal pre-audit for smooth formal approval

### Tariff Optimization

Leveraging Free Trade Agreements (FTAs), preferential rules of origin, and special customs zones to legally reduce duty costs:

- FTA preference utilization rate assessment
- Origin qualification determination
- Transfer pricing and customs valuation coordination
- Processing trade and bonded logistics design

### Trade Compliance Audit

Regular internal compliance audits to identify risks and recommend remediation. Audit scope includes declaration processes, documentation management, internal controls, and staff training.
