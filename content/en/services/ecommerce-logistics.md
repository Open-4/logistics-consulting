## Overseas Warehouse vs Direct Mail

**Direct Mail:** Ship from China directly to buyers via postal/express. Simple, low inventory risk, but 7-15 day delivery and difficult returns. Best for testing new products.

**Overseas Warehouse:** Bulk ship to local warehouse, then deliver domestically. 2-5 day delivery, easier returns, but requires upfront inventory investment. Best for stable-selling products.

## FBA Head Haul

FCL (over 15 CBM) is cheapest but longest (25-30 days to US). LCL suits smaller volumes. Air freight (2-5 days) is 5-10x ocean cost. Express courier (3-7 days) includes customs clearance.

**Important:** Amazon has strict packaging/labeling requirements. Allow 7-day buffer for FBA shipments. Split shipments reduce risk.

## Tax Compliance

**IOSS:** EU simplified VAT scheme for sub-€150 B2C imports. Allows single-point VAT payment.

**VAT:** EU rates 17-27%. Sellers must register and file in destination countries. Non-compliance risks cargo detention and fines.

## Returns Management

Return rates vary by category: clothing 20-30%, electronics 5-10%, home goods 3-5%. Key strategies: local return points, inspection/grade returned goods, resell quality items through refurbished channels.
