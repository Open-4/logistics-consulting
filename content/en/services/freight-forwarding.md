## What is Freight Forwarding

A freight forwarder is a logistics coordinator who manages the transportation of goods on behalf of shippers. Forwarders don''t own ships or planes but act as experts who design optimal shipping solutions.

**Key services:** Booking cargo space, preparing documents, arranging customs clearance, coordinating inland transport, and cargo insurance.

## FCL vs LCL

**FCL (Full Container Load):** Best for shipments over 15 CBM. Your goods occupy an entire container, reducing handling and transit time. Cost per unit is lower for large volumes.

**LCL (Less than Container Load):** For smaller shipments sharing container space. More flexible but takes 3-5 days longer and has more complex pricing (based on volume or weight whichever is greater).

## Air Freight

Fastest option (3-7 days door-to-door) but 5-10x more expensive than ocean. Best for high-value, time-sensitive goods like electronics, pharmaceuticals, and emergency replenishment.

## How to Choose

Consider five factors: cargo value, time sensitivity, volume, destination, and budget. Rail is a good middle-ground option for Europe-bound shipments.
