## Overview

International freight forwarding is an indispensable link in the global supply chain. As a professional bridge between shippers and carriers, freight forwarders not only handle core transportation arrangements but also play a critical strategic role in route optimization, cost control, and risk management.

## Core Services

### Ocean Freight

As the main artery of global trade, our ocean freight services cover Full Container Load (FCL) and Less than Container Load (LCL), including:

- **Route Planning**: Recommend optimal routes based on origin, destination, transit time, and budget
- **Space Booking**: Long-term partnerships with major carriers to secure capacity during peak seasons
- **Documentation**: End-to-end documentation including Bill of Lading, cargo manifest, and packing list
- **Destination Services**: Customs clearance, deconsolidation, warehousing, and last-mile delivery

### Air Freight

For high-value, time-sensitive cargo, air freight offers the fastest transit. Our services cover major global airports:

- **Direct vs. Consolidation**: Optimal routing based on urgency and budget
- **Dangerous Goods**: Full IATA DGR compliance for hazardous materials
- **Charter Services**: Full aircraft charter solutions for urgent bulk shipments
- **Cold Chain**: Temperature-controlled solutions for pharmaceutical and perishable goods

### Rail & Multimodal

With the rapid development of the China-Europe Railway Express, rail has become a vital Asia-Europe logistics corridor. We design sea-rail and road-rail combinations for optimal cost-transit time balance.
