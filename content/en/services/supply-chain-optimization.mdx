## Overview

Supply chain has become a core competitive differentiator in the modern business landscape. We employ a systematic methodology to diagnose and optimize from strategic to operational levels, helping enterprises achieve the optimal balance across cost, efficiency, and resilience.

## Core Services

### Supply Chain Network Design

Evaluate existing network configurations using advanced modeling tools to determine optimal locations and numbers of factories, warehouses, and distribution centers, along with inventory deployment and service coverage parameters.

### Inventory Management & Optimization

Too much inventory ties up capital; too little degrades service. We help build科学的 inventory management systems:

- **Demand Forecasting**: Combine statistical models with market intelligence for improved accuracy
- **Safety Stock Strategy**: Differentiated safety stock levels based on service targets and supply uncertainty
- **ABC-XYZ Analysis**: Sophisticated SKU classification for tailored management
- **Slow-Mover Management**: Early warning and disposition mechanisms

### Procurement & Supplier Management

- **Supplier Tierarchy**: Performance-based supplier evaluation (quality, cost, delivery, service)
- **Strategic Sourcing**: Global supplier discovery and selection
- **Contract Management**: Optimized contract terms to minimize supply risk

### Supply Chain Digitalization

- **Control Tower**: End-to-end supply chain visibility platform
- **Process Automation**: RPA applications in order processing and reconciliation
- **Decision Intelligence**: AI-powered demand forecasting, inventory optimization, and logistics scheduling
